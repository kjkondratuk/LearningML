# Phase 3D: Specialization Tracks

## Overview

Choose one or more specialization tracks based on your interests and career goals.
Each track goes deep into a specific area of ML, with research-level exercises and
a capstone project.

## Available Tracks

| Track | Modules | Focus |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Reinforcement Learning | 7 (6 + capstone) | MDPs, TD learning, policy gradients, PPO, model-based RL, offline RL |
| Computer Vision | 5 (4 + capstone) | Detection, segmentation, ViT, NeRFs |
| ML Systems | 6 (5 + capstone) | Distributed training, quantization, distillation, serving |
| Bayesian ML | 6 (5 + capstone) | GPs, variational inference, MCMC, BDL, Bayesian optimization |

## Prerequisites

- All tracks require completion of Spiral 2 and Phases 3A-3B basics
- **RL track:** Probability, optimization. Self-contained otherwise
- **CV track:** CNNs from Spiral 2, attention from Phase 3B
- **ML Systems track:** Benefits from trained models in 3A/3B to optimize
- **Bayesian ML track:** Benefits from Phase 3C Module 03 (kernels)

## How to Navigate

Pick the track most relevant to your goals. Each track is self-contained and can
be completed independently. You can also do multiple tracks in any order.

Each track culminates in a capstone project that synthesizes the entire track into
a substantial implementation.
