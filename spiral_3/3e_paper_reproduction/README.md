# Phase 3E: Paper Reading & Reproduction

## Overview

This phase teaches you to read, critically evaluate, and reproduce research papers.
It is the culmination of Spiral 3: you will reproduce landmark papers end-to-end and
then design your own extension.

## Prerequisites

- All of Phases 3A-3C (you will use techniques from every earlier phase)
- Comfortable reading LaTeX-formatted mathematics
- Experience training models on real datasets

## Module Index

| # | Module | Focus |
|---|--------|-------|
| 01 | Reading Methodology | How to read papers effectively |
| 02 | Attention Is All You Need | Reproduce Vaswani et al. 2017 (arXiv:1706.03762) |
| 03 | Denoising Diffusion | Reproduce Ho et al. 2020 (arXiv:2006.11239) |
| 04 | Direct Preference Optimization | Reproduce Rafailov et al. 2023 (arXiv:2305.18290) |
| 05 | Capstone: Extend a Paper | Design and execute your own research extension |

## Philosophy

Reproduction is the highest form of understanding. If you can implement a paper from
scratch and reproduce its results, you truly understand the method. These modules push
you beyond implementing isolated components to building complete research pipelines.

## Suggested Order

Start with Module 01 (reading methodology), then tackle papers in order. The capstone
should be done last -- it builds on one of the three reproduced papers.
